This is my personal list of resources I thrown together for those in search of ROMs in the wake of Vimm's no longer being a complete databse.

**Some 1fichier archives may have passwords.** In the event you come across a password, it is probably either `AlvRo` or `_AlvRo`.

If you are a RetroAchievements user, check the "Supported Game Files" button on the game's page to make sure you are using the right ROM.

I haven't uploaded any of these files myself, and have no affiliation with any of the groups linked below. This is simply a compilation of links to other resources.

### Terminology

If you are new to emulation some of these terms may be a bit confusing. Hopefully this helps clear up any confusion you might have.

- AlvRo - A popular ROM archiver's username.
- No-Intro - A group of "ROM auditors" that ensure high quality dumps/rips.
- NoPayStation - Software used to download games, DLC, and updates directly from Sony's CDN, with decryption keys to play them.
- Redump - A group of "ROM auditors" that ensure high quality dumps/rips.
- ROM auditors - Users who ensure ROMs have proper "hashes", aka perfect rips.
- Title Update - A patch/update for a game. Usually good to check if a game has one available.
